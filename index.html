Organisation Size: 100–2,000 Employees
Threshold 01
What This Means
The customer's total active employee headcount falls within 100 to 2,000. This includes all employees on payroll — full time, part time, casual, and contractors on invoice.
Why It Matters
The Tier One fast-track motion is designed for mid-market NZ organisations. Below 100 employees, the integration cost may not justify the investment. Above 2,000, complexity typically increases — multi-entity structures, complex awards, and bespoke requirements become more common.
How to Check
Use CRM data or Datapay records. This information should already be available in your account intelligence — you do not need to ask the customer directly.
